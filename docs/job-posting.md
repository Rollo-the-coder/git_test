## Founding Engineer at WEBA

WEBA is building its first product from the ground up. We're looking for a **founding engineer** to own the codebase, ship our MVP, and help define what engineering looks like here from day one.

### What you'll do

- Build and ship the MVP end-to-end — architecture through deployment
- Own technical decisions with the CEO; push back on scope when simpler paths exist
- Establish engineering practices: code review, testing, CI/CD, and release cadence
- Prepare the codebase and docs so engineer #2 can onboard quickly

### What we're looking for

- **3+ years** shipping production web software (full-stack or strong backend + frontend fluency)
- Comfortable as the **sole engineer** — scoping, building, deploying, and operating
- Strong **product instincts** — you care about user value, not just clean code
- Experience with Git, CI, and cloud deployment
- Clear written communication; async-friendly

### Nice to have

- Prior early-stage or founding-engineer experience
- Experience with agentic or AI-assisted product workflows
- Track record of 0→1 product launches

### What we offer

- **Equity:** 1.0%–3.0% (4-year vest, 1-year cliff) — meaningful ownership as employee #1
- **Salary:** $120k–$160k USD depending on experience and location
- **Impact:** Define the stack, the culture, and the product from zero
- **Path:** Founding Engineer → Lead Engineer → VP Eng as we grow
