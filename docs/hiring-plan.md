## Executive summary

WEBA needs a founding engineer as its first technical hire. This person will own the product codebase end-to-end, ship the MVP, and establish engineering practices that scale with the company. The CEO owns hiring through offer acceptance; this plan defines role scope, sourcing, evaluation, compensation framing, and a 30-day execution timeline.

## Role definition

### Title

Founding Engineer (Employee #1, Engineering)

### Mission

Turn WEBA's product vision into a shippable, maintainable product. Build the MVP, own technical decisions with the CEO, and create the foundation for future engineering hires.

### Core responsibilities

1. **Ship the MVP** — design, implement, and deploy the first customer-facing product.
2. **Own the stack** — select and maintain languages, frameworks, infra, and CI/CD.
3. **Establish engineering norms** — code review, testing, documentation, and release cadence.
4. **Partner with the CEO** — translate roadmap priorities into weekly execution; flag risks early.
5. **Prepare to hire** — document architecture and processes so engineer #2 can onboard quickly.

### Must-have qualifications

- 3+ years building and shipping production web software (full-stack or strong backend with frontend fluency).
- Comfortable as the sole engineer: scoping, building, deploying, and operating without a team.
- Strong product instincts — can push back on scope and propose simpler paths to value.
- Experience with Git, CI, and cloud deployment (any major provider).
- Clear written communication; async-friendly.

### Nice-to-have

- Prior early-stage / founding-engineer experience.
- Experience with agentic or AI-assisted product workflows.
- Prior ownership of 0→1 product launches.

### Anti-patterns (do not hire for)

- Needs heavy management or detailed specs before writing code.
- Only interested in a narrow specialty with no willingness to go full-stack.
- No portfolio or shipped work to discuss.

## Compensation framing

Early-stage founding roles should lead with equity and mission, with cash kept lean until funding or revenue.

| Component | Guideline |
|-----------|-----------|
| Base salary | $120k–$160k USD (adjust for geography and experience) |
| Equity | 1.0%–3.0% vesting over 4 years with 1-year cliff |
| Benefits | Health stipend or PEO once cash allows |
| Title path | Founding Engineer → Lead Engineer → VP Eng (as team grows) |

*Final numbers require CEO/board approval before offer.*

## Interview process

Total target time: **2 weeks from first screen to offer**.

| Stage | Duration | Owner | Pass criteria |
|-------|----------|-------|---------------|
| 1. Intro screen | 30 min | CEO | Mission fit, motivation, comp alignment |
| 2. Technical conversation | 60 min | CEO + advisor (if available) | Depth on past projects, system thinking, tradeoff reasoning |
| 3. Work sample | 3–4 hrs (paid optional) | Candidate | Small scoped task: ship a feature in this repo (see `docs/roadmap.md` Phase 0) |
| 4. Culture / values | 30 min | CEO | Collaboration style, ambiguity tolerance, feedback loops |
| 5. Reference check | async | CEO | 2 references confirm ownership and delivery |

**Work sample brief (use for all candidates):**

> Fork `Rollo-the-coder/git_test`, add a minimal health-check endpoint or CLI, include a README section explaining your approach, and open a PR within 4 hours of starting.

## Success metrics

- **Time-to-offer:** ≤30 days from posting
- **Quality bar:** hire passes all stage criteria; no compromise hires under time pressure

## Risks and mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Thin pipeline | Activate channels 3–5; widen geo; increase equity if needed |
| CEO bandwidth | Batch screens to 2 days/week; use async work sample |
| Comp mismatch | State range upfront in posting; filter early |
| Wrong hire | Paid work sample + references required; no skip |
